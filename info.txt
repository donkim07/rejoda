About Rejoda

Rejoda is a multidisciplinary company dedicated to advancing innovation across technology, healthcare, and commerce in Tanzania. Founded by passionate professionals from both the medical and IT fields, Rejoda delivers integrated solutions ranging from web development and digital systems to pharmaceutical distribution and medical equipment supply.

The company’s name — Rejoda — is derived from the initials of its founders, symbolizing teamwork, trust, and a unified vision to modernize service delivery in Africa.
