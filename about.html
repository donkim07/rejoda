sep 2024 - Foundation

Rejoda was founded with a vision to transform Tanzania through integrated technology, healthcare, and commerce solutions. Our journey began with a small team of passionate innovators committed to making a difference.

  • Company established in Tanzania
  • Initial focus on IT & Digital Solutions
  • First government website projects

oct 2024 - Expansion

We expanded our services to include mobile app development and launched our first major government website network for Pemba region. This year marked our entry into healthcare technology solutions.

  • Launched Pemba Government Network
  • First Android applications deployed
  • Healthcare division established

dec 2024 - Innovation

Innovation took center stage as we developed the Waste Management Portal for Mwanza and Tanga cities. Our e-commerce platform for SKS Pharma Ltd launched, revolutionizing medical product distribution.

  • Waste Management Portal launched
  • SKS Pharma e-commerce platform
  • Multi-city deployment success

jan 2025 - Growth

Significant growth across all divisions. We expanded our pharmaceutical distribution network, enhanced our digital solutions portfolio, and established stronger partnerships across East Africa.

  • 100+ healthcare products distributed
  • Enhanced cloud infrastructure
  • Regional partnerships established

may 2025 - Excellence

We achieved excellence in service delivery, with 10+ government websites live, comprehensive waste management systems operational, and a robust healthcare product distribution network serving communities nationwide.

  • 10+ government websites operational
  • Waste management systems in 3 cities
  • Nationwide healthcare distribution

sep 2025 - Future

Looking ahead, we're focused on AI integration, expanding our East African presence, and developing next-generation digital solutions that will shape the future of technology, healthcare, and commerce in the region.

  • AI & Machine Learning integration
  • East African market expansion
  • Next-gen digital platforms
